Community FAQ

What happens after I report a hotspot?
  • Your report enters the community board.
  • Neighbors can add support to raise urgency.
  • High-priority hotspots route into center dispatch.
What does resident confirmation do?
  • Center marks cleanup complete.
  • Residents confirm if cleanup actually happened.
  • Confirmed closes the event; missed reopens follow-up dispatch.
How are impacts shown here?

Top completed cleanups show location, hotspot type, and ECO-linked impact so residents can track proof-backed neighborhood progress.

How can I help if I am not a recycler?
  • Add your voice to active hotspots.
  • Confirm outcomes honestly after cleanups.
  • Keep report details specific for faster dispatch resolution.
