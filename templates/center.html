Need Help?

What is Queue Discipline and trust-state consistency?

Queue Discipline keeps every event in one trust sequence:

  • Submitted -> Verified -> Anchored / Rewarded / Proof Ready
  • Agent coverage by stage: Collector Agent Active, Verifier Agent Active, Reward Agent Active.
How does this lane protect trust work?
  • Intake: Confirm source lane and recycler identity.
  • Validate: Check material type, weight, and queue context.
  • Finalize: Publish the verified event with reward-ready metadata.

Center verification is the source-of-truth for status, anchoring, and reward progression.

What should I do after resolving a QR intent?
  • Confirm recycler profile auto-loaded correctly.
  • Verify material and measured weight.
  • Submit Verify & Anchor.

The event appears in recent verifications only after attested backend confirmation.

Why is a submitted pickup still pending?

Pending means center validation is not complete yet.

  • Set as Active Pickup.
  • Complete the inspection checklist.
  • Run Verify & Anchor to move it to anchored and reward-ready.
Which checks must pass before verification?
  • Recycler identity verified.
  • No contamination observed.
  • Material-match confirmation complete.
  • Weight capture is realistic and measured.

These checks prevent bad data and keep proof and payout records audit-ready.

How does Token and Proof Handoff map by stage?
  • Submitted: Recycler payload enters center queue.
  • Verified: Center confirms material and weight for payout math.
  • Anchored: Logbook entry is published to Hedera Consensus Service (HCS).
  • Rewarded: ECO payout is finalized through Hedera Token Service (HTS).
  • Proof Ready: Proof bundle links verification, anchors, and reward outcomes.
