How to Redeem EcoCoin for ZAR

Use any Hedera wallet (like HashPack) to swap your tokens.

  1. Import Your Wallet: Go to your profile page and get your private key. Import this account into HashPack.
  2. Associate Token: Add the EcoCoin Token (ID: 0.0.XXXX) to your wallet.
  3. Swap on a DEX: Use a Hedera DEX (like SaucerSwap) to trade your **EcoCoin** for **HBAR** or **USDC**.
  4. Cash Out: From there, you can send your HBAR or USDC to a local exchange (like Luno or VALR) to withdraw it as ZAR to your bank.

Future Plan: Our next step is to partner with a bank to build a direct **EcoCoin -> ZAR** API, making this process instant.
