Our Mission & Impact

VeriCycle is more than an app. It's a new economy for recycling.

❓ The Problem We're Solving

South Africa's recycling system depends on over 90,000 informal waste pickers. These collectors face social stigmatization, severe health risks from handling waste, and economic exploitation from cash-based middlemen. They have no way to build a formal financial history, trapping them in a high-risk, low-reward cycle. At the same time, businesses and communities often lack a reliable way to coordinate and verify recycling activity.

✅ The VeriCycle Solution

VeriCycle connects recyclers, businesses, recycling centers, and communities through a verified recycling network. Recyclers gain rewards and proof of income, businesses gain accountability records, and communities gain tools to coordinate recycling opportunities.

📈 The Real-World Impact

We are building a "Proof of Income" report for informal collectors, breaking the cycle of financial exclusion. VeriCycle expands beyond informal recyclers by allowing businesses to create recycling opportunities and communities to request or confirm pickup, while still keeping recyclers at the center of the economic value chain.
