How VeriCycle Works

A human-centered flow from collection to trusted proof.

1

Request / Collect

A business or neighborhood posts a pickup, or a recycler starts with material already collected and ready for drop-off.

→
2

Drop Off

The recycler brings sorted material to a participating center, where the handoff is captured with weight and source context.

→
3

Center Verification

The recycling center confirms the recycler, material type, and quantity so the event becomes a trusted network record.

→
4

Earn Proof

Rewards flow back to the recycler while the event is preserved as proof of income, proof of diversion, and proof of participation.

Mission & Impact

VeriCycle exists to make recycling work visible, trustworthy, and economically useful for the people doing the labor.

✋

The Problem

Across South Africa, the informal recycling workforce keeps cities cleaner every day, but most of that labor still leaves behind no reliable transaction history. When work stays invisible, people stay locked out of opportunity.

◈

The Solution

VeriCycle records each pickup, drop-off, and verification step as trusted digital proof, turning everyday recycling into recognized work, measurable impact, and a financial story people can build on.

✦

Real-World Impact

That shift changes outcomes on the ground: recyclers build earning history, businesses prove progress, communities track visible cleanup momentum, and local trust grows through every verified handoff.
