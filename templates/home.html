Our Mission & Impact

VeriCycle turns recycling activity into trusted, verifiable proof and fair economic outcomes.

❓ The Problem We're Solving

South Africa's recycling system depends on over 90,000 informal waste pickers. These collectors face social stigmatization, severe health risks from handling waste, and economic exploitation from cash-based middlemen. They have no way to build a formal financial history, trapping them in a high-risk, low-reward cycle. At the same time, businesses and communities often lack a reliable way to coordinate and verify recycling activity.

✅ The VeriCycle Solution

VeriCycle connects recyclers, businesses, recycling centers, and communities through one trust ladder: Submitted, Verified, Anchored, Rewarded, and Proof Ready. Recyclers gain reward outcomes and proof-of-income history, while businesses gain auditable impact records.

📈 The Real-World Impact

We are building a "Proof of Income" report for informal collectors, breaking the cycle of financial exclusion. With anchored records, reward outcomes, and proof bundles, judges and partners can verify impact through visible evidence instead of claims.
