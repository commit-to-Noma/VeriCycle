Need Help?

How do I get paid in EcoCoin?

After your drop-off is verified, rewards are credited instantly to your Rewards Wallet.

Why is my pickup marked 'Pending'?

Pending means the center still needs to verify weight/material before payout is finalized.

What is the 'Proof Flow'?

Submitted → Verified → Anchored → Rewarded. This chain creates auditable proof records.

What materials are accepted?

Paper, cardboard, cans, glass, plastics, and mixed recyclables depending on partner intake rules.

How is my EcoCoin calculated?

1 EcoCoin is rewarded for every 1 kg of verified material you drop off.
