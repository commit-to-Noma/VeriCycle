Need Help?

How do I get paid in EcoCoin?

After your drop-off is verified, rewards are credited instantly to your Rewards Wallet.

Why is my pickup marked 'Pending'?

Pending means the center still needs to verify weight/material before payout is finalized.

What is the Token and Proof Flow?

1. Accepted: Recycler secures the pickup assignment.

2. In Transit: Recycler has collected materials and is en route.

3. Submitted: Drop-off is sent to center verification queue.

4. Verified: Center validates weight/material and creates activity.

5. Anchored + Rewarded: Hedera logbook (HCS) and reward transfer (HTS) finalize proof.

What materials are accepted?

Paper & Cardboard, Plastics, Glass, Metals, E-Waste, Organic Waste, and Mixed Recyclables.

How is my EcoCoin calculated?

Payout is simple: verified kg x live ECO/kg rate for that material.

Example: Plastics 10kg x 5.0 ECO/kg = 50 ECO (~R50).

EcoCoin includes a premium vs street cash yards, typically +15% to +50% depending on material.
