Need Help?

Business FAQ

How do I read the lifecycle status cards?
  • Submitted -> Verified -> Anchored -> Rewarded -> Proof Ready.
  • Each label shows exactly where a request sits in the trust flow.
What does each trust pipeline step mean?
  • Submitted: request created and awaiting pickup.
  • In Transit: recycler collected and en route.
  • Verified: center confirms material and weight.
  • Anchored: proof recorded on Hedera.
  • Rewarded: ECO transferred to recycler.
How do I create a request that recyclers can accept quickly?
  • Set a clear material type.
  • Use a realistic kg estimate.
  • Add a specific pickup window.

Requests with complete details are accepted faster.

What does "Submitted" mean on my request?

The recycler has dropped off material and the center is validating weight and material before final trust-state progression.

When does a record become Proof Ready?

After verification and anchoring complete. Then proof links and Hedera references appear in your verified events table.

How does my spend create local impact?
  • Business demand funds verified collection activity.
  • The same records power ESG reporting.
  • Reward value flows to local recyclers.
Where do Verified ESG Impact metrics come from and how are they calculated?
  • Source data comes from verified pickup records in your Business and Center flow (weight, status, and anchored proof references).
  • Environmental metric uses verified kilograms converted to CO2e with the platform factor: CO2e averted = verified kg x 1.85.
  • Social metric uses funded EcoCoin flow: micro-jobs funded = ceil(ECO funded / 200).

During pitch recording mode, this page can show fixed hero values for consistency, while live mode computes from current verified transactions.
